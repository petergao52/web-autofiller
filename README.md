6. click save and continue again
7. For what is your base salary expectation? Type 150000.

Are you legally authorized to work in the country / one of the countries where this position is located?* Select Yes option
How many years of experience do you have in roles or projects that closely align with the responsibilities outlined in the job description?* Select 10 or more years option
